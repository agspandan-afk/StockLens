🔧 Under the hood — how we calculated this ▼ show

How the backtest works: We simulate three different strategies on your historical data — each one using a different "lookback window" to match short, medium, and long-term trading. For each simulated trade, we record whether price went up or down. From those results we compute win rate, average gain, and average loss — then run them through the expectancy formula E = ((1 + avgWin/avgLoss) × winRate) − 1 to find whether there was a real edge. Position size is calculated using the Kelly Criterion. All of this is based on past data only — past performance does not guarantee future results.
